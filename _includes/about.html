About

The workshops on Generic Attacks and Proofs in Symmetric Cryptography bring together leading experts and early-career researchers in the field of provable security for symmetric-key cryptography to discuss recent advances, revisit long-standing open problems, and identify promising directions for future research.
In addition to the in-person workshops, we host a recurring seminar, Online GAPS, to provide a platform to share new results, exchange ideas, and encourage collaborations, especially among PhD students and early-career researchers.
